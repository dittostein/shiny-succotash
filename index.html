Controls

  • ←/→ move sideways
  • ↓ ride the updraft (fast upward)
  • ↑ rotate
  • Space slam upward
  • P pause or resume

How it works

New pieces spawn near the bottom and drift toward the sky. When a row is filled, it disappears and fresh space opens beneath the tower. Keep the stack from breaching the ceiling.
